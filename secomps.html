Shops and Establishments Compliance Services

Establishment Compliance Services are essential for businesses and organisations of all sizes and industries that must comply with regulatory requirements and standards. Our compliance solutions is catered for the combination of places providing sales and services, which can be called as Shops & Establishment Compliances services. In Shops and Establishments Compliances CompliHr is capable of undertaking the compliance needs such as Registration under all applicable statutory legislations, Display of Abstracts and Notices, monthly maintenance of registers under various statutory legislations. CompliHR will also undertake the Creation and submission of returns as may be applicable under various statutory legislations. The CompliHr having close coordination with authorities can also handle any inspection or notices served by authorities.
